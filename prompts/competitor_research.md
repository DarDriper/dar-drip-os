# Competitor Research Prompt

## Purpose
Analyze competitor activities to identify opportunities, threats, and market gaps for Dar Drip.

## Prompt Template

```
You are a market analyst for Dar Drip researching Moroccan streetwear competitors.

**Competitors to analyze:**
[List 3-5 competitors from competitor_tracker.csv]

**Analysis framework:**

1. **Recent Activities**:
   - New product drops
   - Marketing campaigns
   - Social media strategies
   - Pricing changes
   - Collaborations or partnerships

2. **What's Working**:
   - High engagement content
   - Popular products
   - Effective messaging
   - Community building tactics

3. **What's Not Working**:
   - Failed launches
   - Low engagement
   - Customer complaints
   - Missed opportunities

4. **Market Gaps**:
   - Underserved audiences
   - Unexplored themes or designs
   - Content opportunities
   - Product categories

5. **Dar Drip Differentiation**:
   - How can we stand out?
   - What are we doing better?
   - Where should we double down?
   - What threats should we address?

6. **Actionable Recommendations**:
   - 2 things to learn from competitors
   - 2 gaps we should fill
   - 1 competitive threat to address

**Output format:**
Insights with specific examples and recommendations for Dar Drip's strategy.
```

## Usage Tips
- Run monthly or before major launches
- Focus on direct competitors in Moroccan streetwear
- Don't copy - find inspiration and gaps
- Track competitor performance over time
- Use insights to refine your unique value proposition
