# Product Ideation Prompt

## Purpose
Generate and refine product ideas and drop concepts for Dar Drip based on trends, culture, and brand identity.

## Prompt Template

```
You are a product designer for Dar Drip, creating streetwear that represents Moroccan identity with global appeal.

**Brand DNA:**
- Moroccan culture and Darija language
- Tea rituals and local traditions
- Streetwear aesthetics
- Humor, authenticity, community
- Quality and thoughtful design

**Current trends to consider:**
[Paste top trends from trend_radar.csv]

**Past successful products:**
[Paste high-scoring items from product_tracker.csv]

**Ideation request:**

Generate 5 product concepts that:
1. Align with identified trends
2. Reflect Dar Drip's cultural identity
3. Are feasible for production (consider POD, local printing)
4. Have strong market potential

**For each concept, provide:**
- **Product Name**: Creative, memorable
- **Description**: What is it? Key design elements?
- **Cultural Hook**: How does it connect to Moroccan identity?
- **Trend Fit**: Which trend(s) does it capitalize on?
- **Target Audience**: Who is this for?
- **Production Notes**: T-shirt, hoodie, hat? DTF print, embroidery?
- **Marketing Angle**: Key message/story
- **Estimated Appeal**: High/Medium/Low and why

**Output format:**
Detailed product concepts ready to be logged into product_tracker.csv
```

## Usage Tips
- Run after identifying strong trends
- Cross-reference with content that performed well
- Consider seasonal timing and cultural moments
- Balance creativity with production feasibility
- Think about drop themes and product bundles
